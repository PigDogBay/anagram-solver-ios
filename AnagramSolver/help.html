Anagram Solver Help


Anagram Solver is a powerful word search engine helping you to find anagrams, solve crosswords and much more

The app uses its own built in word list of over 130,000 words (280,000+ words in the Pro version).



Anagrams

Type in the letters you have, press Search to find all the possible words that can be made from those letters.

Enter the letters steam, to find:

mates
meats
tames
tames
teams


Sub-Anagrams

Find words within words. For example, enter a nine letter word and find all the possible eight, seven, six...

Enter the word hearts, to find:

earth (s)
share (t)
rate (sh)
she (art)


Two Word Anagrams

Enter manchester united to find:

mechanised nutter
inducement haters

The order of the letters does not matter, the space determines the size of the two words to search for.


Missing Letters

To solve crossword clues use dots . for the missing letters.

Enter c..v.r to find:

clever
clover
carver


Shortcuts

Use numbers to represent missing letters.

Enter z9 to find:

zookeepers
zombielike


Wildcard

Use @ to represent 1 or more missing letters.

Enter @ace to find:

face
hyperspace
peace


Supergrams

Find larger words that contain your anagram letters.

Enter microsoft* to find:

discomforting
nonconformists
oversimplification

Using * will search all words, you can restrict word size using +

Enter google++++ to find all ten letter words containing eggloo:

bootlegger
boondoggle
egyptology

Cryptic crosswords may give you only a few letters of the anagram, the supergram feature is very handy in these cases.


Note that Supergrams are only available in the Pro version of the app


Dictionary Definition

To the right of a word is an information icon, press to look up the word's meaning on the internet.


Share With Friends

On the results screen in the top right corner is the share icon, press to the send results to your friends via email.


Go Pro

Upgrade the app to the Pro version, you get
  • Bigger word list, 280,000 words instead of 130,000
  • Supergram searches
  • No Ads - more screen space

To Go Pro, press the menu button in the top left of the screen and select Go Pro from the menu. An In App Purchase is required.
